Common Questions

Seller FAQs

I provide a comprehensive comparative market analysis (CMA) that looks at recent sales of similar homes in your area, current market conditions, and your home's unique features. Use the valuation tool above for a quick estimate, then reach out for a detailed, personalized analysis.
Not every improvement yields a return. I typically recommend focusing on decluttering, deep cleaning, fresh paint in neutral tones, minor repairs, and curb appeal. Kitchen and bathroom updates can add value, but major renovations before selling may not always pay off. I'll walk through your home and give specific recommendations.
The time to sell varies based on market conditions, location, price, and condition. In a seller's market, homes can sell within days. In a balanced market, expect 30-60 days on average. Proper pricing and staging are the most important factors in selling quickly. I'll develop a strategy tailored to your timeline.
Your net proceeds depend on the sale price minus your remaining mortgage, agent commissions, closing costs, and any repairs or credits. Use the net proceeds calculator above to get a quick estimate, then contact me for a detailed breakdown based on your situation.
Staging involves arranging furniture and decor to showcase your home's best features. Studies consistently show that staged homes sell faster and for higher prices — often 1-5% more. I work with professional stagers and can advise on whether full staging, partial staging, or virtual staging makes sense for your property.
